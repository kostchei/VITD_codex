# Shadowdark Unified Rules Reference

This document compiles the player rules (from pages 1-68 of the *Shadowdark Player Quickstart - Digital.pdf*) and the Game Master rules (from pages 2 and 10–17 of the *Shadowdark Game Master Quickstart - Digital.pdf*).



---

## 1. Rules Reference & Tables

### A. Core Ethos & Master Rules
* **The Only Rule:** The GM makes the rules. The book is a guide, not a constraint; nothing takes precedence over the GM's judgment.
* **The Pact:** Fellowship, goodwill, and respect between GM and players. Decisions that defend The Pact (fun and support) are always good ones.
* **Core Principles:**
  * **Time:** The most important resource. Must haunt every decision; players cannot search every tile.
  * **Darkness:** The true foe. Keep lighting tools precious and limited.
  * **Gear:** Precious and limited. Every slot and tool must have value and utility.
  * **Action Economy:** One action and one movement per turn for PCs. Make it count.
  * **Information:** Dispense information freely. If players test/search a spot where a trap is, they find it automatically.
  * **Distance:** Loose measurements (Close = 5 feet, Near = up to 30 feet, Far = within sight). Precision is not required.
  * **Danger:** Risky spells, volatile magic, fast and unfair fights, insidious monsters.
  * **Rewarding Investment:** Actions, languages, and titles earned must have weight and impact.
  * **Checks:** Automatic success for trained tasks. Roll *only* when there is time pressure, failure has dire consequences, and the task requires skill.

#### 3. Ancestries
* **Dwarf:** Stout (+2 HP. Roll hit points per level with advantage). Knows Common and Dwarvish.
* **Elf:** Farsight (+1 bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons or +1 bonus to spellcasting checks). Knows Common, Elvish, and Sylvan.
* **Goblin:** Keen Senses (cannot be surprised). Knows Common and Goblin.
* **Halfling:** Stealthy (once per day, can become invisible for 3 rounds). Knows Common.
* **Half-Orc:** Mighty (+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with melee weapons). Knows Common and Orcish.
* **Human:** Ambitious (gains one additional talent roll at 1st level). Knows Common and one additional common language.

#### 4. Classes
* **Fighter:**
  * **Weapons:** All weapons.
  * **Armor:** All armor and shields.
  * **Hit Points:** 1d8 per level.
  * **Hauler:** Add your Constitution modifier, if positive, to your gear slots.
  * **Weapon Mastery:** Choose one type of weapon (e.g., longswords). You gain +1 to attack and damage with that weapon type. In addition, add half your level to these rolls (round down).
  * **Grit:** Choose Strength or Dexterity. You have advantage on checks of that type to overcome an opposing force (e.g., kicking open a stuck door for STR, or escaping chains for DEX).
* **Priest:**
  * **Weapons:** Club, crossbow, dagger, mace, longsword, staff, warhammer.
  * **Armor:** All armor and shields.
  * **Hit Points:** 1d6 per level.
  * **Languages:** Celestial, Diabolic, or Primordial.
  * **Turn Undead:** Knows the *Turn Undead* spell (doesn't count toward known spells).
  * **Deity:** Serves a god matching alignment. Receives a holy symbol (takes 0 gear slots).
  * **Spellcasting:** Casts priest spells. Starts with two tier 1 spells.
* **Thief:**
  * **Weapons:** Club, crossbow, dagger, shortbow, shortsword.
  * **Armor:** Leather armor, mithral chainmail.
  * **Hit Points:** 1d4 per level.
  * **Backstab:** If hitting a creature unaware of the attack, deal +1 weapon die of damage. Add additional weapon dice equal to half your level (round down).
  * **Thievery:** Secretes tools (0 gear slots). Trained and has advantage on checks for: Climbing, Sneaking/hiding, Applying disguises, Finding/disabling traps, Delicate tasks (picking pockets, opening locks).
* **Wizard:**
  * **Weapons:** Dagger, staff.
  * **Armor:** None.
  * **Hit Points:** 1d4 per level.
  * **Languages:** Knows 2 additional common and 2 rare languages.
  * **Learning Spells:** Can study a scroll for a day and succeed on a DC 15 INT check to permanently learn it. The scroll is expended either way.
  * **Spellcasting:** Casts wizard spells. Starts with three tier 1 spells.

#### 7. Level Advancement
To gain a level, you need to earn your `current level * 10 XP`. Upon reaching a new level, total XP resets to 0. Maximum HP increases by rolling your class's HP die, and you gain a talent roll at levels 1, 3, 5, 7, and 9.

### C. Difficulty Classes, Modifiers, & Checks

#### 1. Standard DCs
* **Easy (DC 9):** Leaping narrow chasm, sneaking past inattentive guard.
* **Normal (DC 12):** Kicking open stuck door, picking a poor lock.
* **Hard (DC 15):** Swimming rough water, stabilizing a dying character.
* **Extreme (DC 18):** Climbing slippery cliff one-handed, restraining a lion.

#### 2. Checks
Roll `1d20 + Stat Modifier`. If the total is equal to or beats the DC, you succeed.
* **Contested Checks:** When multiple creatures work against each other, both roll. Highest result wins (reroll ties).
* **When to Roll:** Roll only when there is time pressure, the action requires skill, and failure has negative consequences. Social checks are generally bypassed by good player roleplay.

### D. Advantage, Disadvantage, & Luck

* **Advantage:** Roll two d20s and use the higher result.
* **Disadvantage:** Roll two d20s and use the lower result.
* **Canceling:** If both apply, they cancel out and you roll a single d20.
* **Natural 20:** Automatic hit (critical hit) in combat, or maximum capability success.
* **Natural 1:** Automatic miss in combat, or critical failure.
* **The D6 Decider:** Roll 1d6 when a random binary outcome is needed. 1–3 represents the worse outcome, 4–6 represents the better outcome.
* **Luck Tokens:**
  * Awarded by the GM for heroic, cool, or creative actions.
  * A player can hold at most 1 luck token at a time.
  * Spend a luck token to reroll any roll. Must accept the new result. Can also give the token to a companion.

---

### E. Time, Initiative, & Turn Order

* **Real Time:** Time passes in the game world at the same pace as real life. 1 hour real time = 1 hour game time = 10 rounds. This is critical for tracking light sources.
* **Initiative:** At the start of play, everyone rolls `1d20 + DEX modifier`. The GM rolls once using the highest modifier of the monsters. Play starts with the highest roll and proceeds clockwise.
* **Action Economy:**
  * **Player Turn:** (1) Count down personal timers. (2) Take 1 action and move up to Near (can split, or skip action to move Near again). (3) GM describes results.
  * **GM Turn:** (1) Count down GM-tracked timers. (2) Check for random encounters. (3) Take monster actions. (4) Describe what PCs notice.

### F. Crawling, Light, & Movement

* **Light Sources:** Last 1 hour of real time. Illuminate to Near (lanterns to double Near). Only one timer runs; lighting a new light source adds onto or resets the timer.
* **Total Darkness:** Non-darkness-adapted creatures have disadvantage on most tasks. Environment becomes deadly; check for random encounters *every round*.
* **Campfires:** Combining three torches creates a stationary campfire. Lasts up to 8 hours (provided someone stays near) and casts light to Near range.
* **Climbing:** DEX or STR check. Move at half speed. Fall if check fails by 5 or more points.
* **Falling:** Take 1d6 damage for every 10 feet fallen.
* **Swimming:** Swim at half speed. STR check in rough water. Hold breath for CON modifier rounds (minimum 1). Thereafter, make a CON check each round or take 1d6 damage.
* **Moving Through:** Pass freely through allies. Make a STR or DEX check to move through enemies.
* **Regrouping:** During crawling, the GM can allow PCs to form a marching order and move as a group, taking one round per movement.

### G. Stealth & Surprise

* **Stealth Check:** Roll Dexterity check vs. passive searcher or a Wisdom check from an active searcher.
* **Hiding Limitations:** Cannot hide while in direct sight.
* **Surprise:** Undetected creatures get one turn before combat initiative is rolled. Attacks against surprised targets have advantage. Attacking reveals the attacker's position.

---

### H. Combat & Death

* **Attacks:**
  * **Melee Attack:** Roll `1d20 + STR modifier + bonuses` vs. target AC.
  * **Ranged Attack:** Roll `1d20 + DEX modifier + bonuses` vs. target AC.
* **Critical Hit:** On a natural 20, double weapon damage dice, or double one numerical spell effect.
* **Knockout:** Can choose to knock a target unconscious instead of killing it when reducing it to 0 HP.
* **Terrain:** Cover (half body obscured) grants disadvantage to attacks. Full cover prevents targeting. Rough terrain halves movement speed.
* **Morale:** Enemies flee if they fail a DC 15 WIS check when reduced to half their group size (or half HP for a solo enemy). Large groups make one roll using the leader's WIS modifier.
* **Death & Dying:**
  * At 0 HP, a character is unconscious and dying.
  * **Death Timer:** Roll `1d4 + CON modifier` (minimum 1 round). The PC dies in that many rounds unless healed or stabilized.
  * **Turn Roll:** On each subsequent turn, the player rolls a d20. A natural 20 wakes the character with 1 HP.
  * **Stabilize:** A close ally can make a DC 15 INT check. Success stops the dying process, but the target remains unconscious at 0 HP.

### I. Spellcasting Mechanics

* **Spellcasting Check:** Roll `1d20 + INT modifier` (Wizard) or `+ WIS modifier` (Priest).
* **Target DC:** `10 + Spell Tier`.
* **Failure:** The spell fails and cannot be cast again until the caster completes a rest.
* **Critical Success (Natural 20):** Double one of the spell's numerical effects. For focus spells, this lasts until the next focus check.
* **Critical Failure (Natural 1):**
  * **Wizard:** Spell is lost until rest, and must roll on the Wizard Mishap table.
  * **Priest:** Deity revokes the spell. The priest cannot cast it again until they perform penance (quest or sacrifice of item/gold) and rest.
* **Focus Spells:**
  * You cannot cast other focus spells while focusing.
  * At the start of your turn, make a focus check (spellcasting check). Failure ends the spell (but doesn't lose it). Critical failure treats it as a standard critical failure.
  * Distraction or damage triggers an immediate focus check.

### K. Spell Catalog
Detailed descriptions of all Tier 1 and Tier 2 spells.

#### CURE WOUNDS
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** Instant

Your touch restores ebbing life. Roll a number of d6s equal to 1 + half your level (rounded down). One target you touch regains that many hit points.

#### HOLY WEAPON
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

One weapon you touch is imbued with a sacred blessing. The weapon becomes magical and has +1 to attack and damage rolls for the duration.

#### SHIELD OF FAITH
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

A protective force wrought of your holy conviction surrounds you. You gain a +2 bonus to your armor class for the duration.

#### TURN UNDEAD
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Instant

You rebuke undead creatures, forcing them to flee. You must present a holy symbol to cast this spell. Undead creatures within near of you must make a CHA check vs. your spellcasting check. If a creature fails by 10+ points and is equal to or less than your level, it is destroyed. Otherwise, on a fail, it flees from you for 5 rounds.

#### AUGURY
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** Instant

You interpret the meaning of supernatural portents and omens. Ask the GM one question about a specific course of action. The GM says whether the action will lead to “weal” or “woe.”

#### BLESS
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** Instant

One creature you touch gains a luck token.

#### BLIND/DEAFEN
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Focus

You utter a divine censure, blinding or deafening one creature you can see in range. The creature has disadvantage on tasks requiring the lost sense.

#### CLEANSING WEAPON
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

One weapon you touch is wreathed in purifying flames. It deals an additional 1d4 damage (1d6 vs. undead) for the duration.

#### SMITE
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Instant

You call down punishing flames on a creature you can see within range. It takes 1d6 damage.

#### ZONE OF TRUTH
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Priest
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Focus

You compel a creature you can see to speak truth. It can’t utter a deliberate lie while within range.

#### LIGHT
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Priest, Wizard
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** 1 hour real time

One object you touch glows with bright, heatless light, illuminating out to a near distance for 1 hour of real time.

#### PROTECTION FROM EVIL
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Priest, Wizard
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** Focus

For the spell’s duration, chaotic beings have disadvantage on attack rolls and hostile spellcasting checks against the target. These beings also can’t possess, compel, or beguile it. When cast on an already- possessed target, the possessing entity makes a CHA check vs. the last spellcasting check. On a failure, the entity is expelled.

#### ALARM
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** 1 day

You touch one object, such as a door threshold, setting a magical alarm on it. If any creature you do not designate while casting the spell touches or crosses past the object, a magical bell sounds in your head.

#### BURNING HANDS
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** Instant

You spread your fingers with thumbs touching, unleashing a circle of flame that fills a close area around where you stand. Creatures within the area of effect take 1d6 damage. Unattended flammable objects ignite.

#### CHARM PERSON
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** 1d8 days

You magically beguile one humanoid of level 2 or less within near range, who regards you as a friend for the duration. The spell ends if you or your allies do anything to hurt it that it notices. The target knows you magically enchanted it after the spell ends.

#### DETECT MAGIC
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Focus

You can sense the presence of magic within near range for the spell's duration. If you focus for two rounds, you discern its general properties. Full barriers block this spell.

#### FEATHER FALL
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** Instant

You may make an attempt to cast this spell when you fall. Your rate of descent slows so that you land safely on your feet.

#### FLOATING DISK
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** 10 rounds

You create a floating, circular disk of force with a concave center. It can carry up to 20 gear slots. It hovers at waist level and automatically stays within near of you. It can’t cross over drop- offs or pits taller than a human.

#### HOLD PORTAL
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** 10 rounds

You magically hold a portal closed for the duration. A creature must make a successful STR check vs. your spellcasting check to open the portal. The knock spell ends this spell.

#### MAGE ARMOR
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** 10 rounds

An invisible layer of magical force protects your vitals. Your armor class becomes 14 (18 on a critical spellcasting check) for the spell’s duration.

#### MAGIC MISSILE
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Far
* **Duration:** Instant

You have advantage on your check to cast this spell. A glowing bolt of force streaks from your open hand, dealing 1d4 damage to one target.

#### SLEEP
* **Tier:** 1
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Instant

You weave a lulling spell that fills a near-sized cube extending from you. Living creatures in the area of effect fall into a deep sleep if they are LV 2 or less. Vigorous shaking or being injured wakes them.

#### ACID ARROW
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Far
* **Duration:** Focus

You conjure a corrosive bolt that hits one foe, dealing 1d6 damage a round. The bolt remains in the target for as long as you focus.

#### ALTER SELF
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

You magically change your physical form, gaining one feature that modifies your existing anatomy. For example, you can grow functional gills on your neck or bear claws on your fingers. This spell can’t grow wings or limbs.

#### DETECT THOUGHTS
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Focus

You peer into the mind of one creature you can see within the spell’s range. Each round, you learn the target’s immediate thoughts. On its turn, the target makes a Wisdom check vs. your last spellcasting check. If the target succeeds, it notices your presence in its mind and the spell ends.

#### FIXED OBJECT
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

An object you touch that weighs no more than 5 pounds becomes fixed in its current location. It can support up to 5,000 pounds of weight for the duration of the spell.

#### HOLD PERSON
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Focus

You magically paralyze one humanoid creature of LV 4 or less you can see within range.

#### INVISIBILITY
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Close
* **Duration:** 10 rounds

A creature you touch becomes invisible for the spell’s duration. The spell ends if the target attacks or casts a spell.

#### KNOCK
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Near
* **Duration:** Instant

A door, window, gate, chest, or portal you can see within range instantly opens, defeating all mundane locks and barriers. This spell creates a loud knock audible to all within earshot.

#### LEVITATE
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** Focus

You can float a near distance vertically per round on your turn. You can also push against solid objects to move horizontally.

#### MIRROR IMAGE
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

You create a number of illusory duplicates of yourself equal to half your level rounded down (minimum 1). The duplicates surround you and mimic you. Each time a creature attacks you, the attack misses and causes one of the duplicates to evaporate. If all of the illusions have disappeared, the spell ends.

#### MISTY STEP
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Self
* **Duration:** Instant

In a puff of smoke, you teleport a near distance to an area you can see.

#### SILENCE
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Far
* **Duration:** Focus

You magically mute sound in a near cube within the spell’s range. Creatures inside the area are deafened, and any sounds they create cannot be heard.

#### WEB
* **Tier:** 2
* **Class:** Wizard
* **Range:** Far
* **Duration:** 5 rounds

You create a near-sized cube of sticky, dense spider web within the spell’s range. A creature stuck in the web can’t move and must succeed on a Strength check vs. your spellcasting check to free itself.
